SOA UNIVERSITY

Siksha ‘O’ Anusandhan University (SOA), Bhubaneswar is 20th in the University category, 34th in the Engineering category, 23rd in Medical Colleges and 12th in Dental category by MHRD NIRF India Rankings 2020. SOA offers undergraduate, postgraduate and doctoral courses in the discipline of Engineering, Pharmacy, Management, etc. Admission to Siksha ‘O’ Anusandhan is done on the basis of national and university entrance test scores. The candidates can apply through online mode, Apart from academics, around 30 percent of these students have received job offers during the Day Zero dream campus drive
